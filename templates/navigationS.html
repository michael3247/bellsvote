For Student Users

As a Student, you can participate in elections, view your account details, and vote for candidates in active elections.

1. Login

Login using either your email or matric number:

  • Email Login
  • Matric Number Login

2. Dashboard (Student)

Once logged in, you will be redirected to the Student Dashboard, where you can access the following:

  • Active Elections: View and participate in ongoing elections by selecting candidates to vote for. You can also see the election's post, start, and end time.
  • Vote for Candidates: In the "Active Elections" section, you can vote for candidates. You can unvote if you wish to change your vote before the election ends.
  • View Results: Access election results through the "Results" link. View vote counts and winners for each completed election.

3. Account Control

Students can view and manage their account details:

  • Logout: Log out by clicking "Logout" in your account settings.
  • Delete Account: You can request to delete your account from the "Account" page. A confirmation prompt will ensure this action is intentional.

Common Features for Both Admins and Students

Navigation Bar

The navigation bar provides quick access to key sections of the portal:

  • Dashboard: Admins see their control panel; students view active elections.
  • Active Elections: View and vote in ongoing elections.
  • Results: View results of completed elections.
  • Account: View and manage your account settings.

Time Format for Elections

Elections have a clear start and end time. You can only vote during the active period. Once voting ends, no further votes can be cast.

Important Notes:

  • Election Integrity: Ensure you vote only during the active election period.
  • Voting Restriction: Each user can vote only once per election.
  • Data Privacy: User data, including votes, is secure and kept confidential.
